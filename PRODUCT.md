# Product Philosophy

## Vision

**"A realtime cultural prediction exchange where crowd sentiment becomes market probability."**

Market Predict transforms the way people engage with cultural events. We're building a platform where collective intelligence meets entertainment, creating a new form of social forecasting.

## What We Are

- **A Prediction Market**: Where probabilities reflect collective wisdom
- **A Cultural Forecasting Engine**: Tracking the pulse of entertainment
- **A Crowd-Intelligence System**: Aggregating insights from fans and analysts
- **A Realtime Probability Platform**: Live updates as sentiment shifts

## What We Are NOT

- ❌ A casino
- ❌ A gambling site
- ❌ A sports betting platform
- ❌ A financial speculation tool

## Core Principles

### 1. Intelligence Over Luck

Every market is backed by data. AI-generated priors provide a starting point, and crowd trading refines the probability. The platform rewards knowledge, not chance.

### 2. Entertainment First

This is about cultural engagement. Users participate because they're passionate about K-POP, movies, dramas, and trends—not because they want to gamble.

### 3. Transparency

- Clear probability calculations
- Visible AI confidence scores
- Open trading history
- Explained market resolutions

### 4. Accessibility

- Mobile-first design
- Simple YES/NO markets
- Clear visual indicators
- No complex trading interfaces

## Market Design

### Market Structure

Every market follows a simple pattern:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────┐
│  [Image/Media]                      │
│                                     │
│  Will BTS's new album sell          │
│  1M copies in the first week?       │
│                                     │
│  ┌─────────────┐  ┌─────────────┐   │
│  │   YES 67%   │  │   NO 33%    │   │
│  └─────────────┘  └─────────────┘   │
│                                     │
│  🔥 Trending  •  🤖 AI: 72%        │
└─────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Probability Display

- Large, readable percentage
- Color coding (green for high YES, red for high NO)
- Movement indicators (↑↓)
- Confidence bands

### AI Integration

- AI prior shown alongside market price
- Confidence score visible
- AI vs Crowd comparison
- Explanation available on tap

## Guiding Questions

When making product decisions, ask:

1. **Does this make the platform more engaging?**
2. **Does this help users make better predictions?**
3. **Does this maintain our non-gambling identity?**
4. **Does this work on mobile?**
5. **Is this simple enough for casual users?**
6. **Does this respect user intelligence?**
